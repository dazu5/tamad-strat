## Verdict

**The naked pattern has no edge.** Across 97,535 trades, win rates sit in a tight band
around the 1:3 breakeven of 25% (22.9%-26.1%); aggregate expectancy is ~0 R and PFs
straddle 1.0 (0.889-1.062). At these sample sizes the confidence intervals are under
+/-1 point, so this is a precise zero, not an ambiguous one: V0 as taught, at zero
cost, is a coin flip - and any real spread/fees would make it a steady loser.

Against the group's claimed 60% at 1:3: the mechanical rules alone produce ~25%.
Whatever drives the journaled 60% is NOT the bare color pattern - candidates are the
significant-area context (V1.x/V2, issues #8-#10), the pattern pills (#7), and the
discretionary layer the group applies beyond the written rules.

Clues worth carrying forward (thin but consistent):
- 15m is the WORST timeframe for all three symbols; 5m carries the best PFs
  (SOL 5m 1.054, ETH 5m 1.014, BTC 5m 1.003). Note this inverts the TJR finding.
- Shorts modestly beat longs (PF 1.019 vs 0.985 - 48k trades each).
- Asia hours (00-08 UTC) beat US hours (PF 1.022 vs 0.987).
- SOL is the friendliest asset (both its 5m and 1h are the only PFs above 1.05).
None of these clear a tradeable bar alone; they are directions for the pills, zones,
and ablation phases, selected on training data only.
